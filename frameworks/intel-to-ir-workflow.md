# Intelligence-to-Incident Response Workflow

## Purpose
This framework illustrates how cyber threat intelligence (CTI) is operationalized to support incident response, cyber risk prioritization, and executive decision-making during active cyber incidents.

---

## Phase 1: Intelligence Collection
- Open-source intelligence (OSINT) and industry reporting
- Dark web monitoring and cybercriminal forum analysis
- Incident artifacts (alerts, logs, authentication anomalies)
- Vendor and partner intelligence feeds

**Manager focus:** defining intelligence requirements rather than collecting indiscriminately.

---

## Phase 2: Validation and Contextualization
- Correlate incident indicators with known threat actor tradecraft
- Validate intelligence relevance and timeliness
- De-prioritize low-confidence or non-actionable data

**Outcome:** reduced noise and faster decision-making.

---

## Phase 3: Incident Triage Support
- Assess likelihood of escalation (e.g., ransomware, fraud)
- Identify at-risk systems and business functions
- Inform incident severity classification and escalation thresholds

**CTI enables prioritization**, not just situational awareness.

---

## Phase 4: Containment and Mitigation Guidance
- Recommend targeted containment actions aligned to adversary behavior
- Support proactive defense against likely next-stage techniques
- Align intelligence findings with incident response playbooks

---

## Phase 5: Executive Communication
- Translate technical findings into business impact
- Provide concise risk posture summaries
- Present decision options and associated trade-offs

Executives receive clarity, not raw data.

---

## Phase 6: Feedback and Intelligence Refinement
- Incorporate incident outcomes into intelligence requirements
- Adjust collection priorities based on effectiveness
- Document lessons learned to improve future response

This closes the intelligence lifecycle loop.

---

## Value to the Organization
- Faster containment decisions
- Reduced escalation risk
- Improved cross-functional alignment
- Intelligence-driven incident response maturity
